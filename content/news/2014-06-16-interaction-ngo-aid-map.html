InterAction launches NGO Aid Map June 16, 2014    

InterAction launched its global NGO Aid Map a platform that visualizes where InterAction’s members work and what they do around the world using maps and other data visualizations. With over 180 members around the world, InterAction is the largest alliance of U.S.-based international NGOs, and was the first civil society group to endorse the Partnership.
